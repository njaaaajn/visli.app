The Idea

Visli is a digital life archive – made up of many small, completed moments. Not endless tracking, not optimization at all costs, but conscious preservation of what matters to you right now.

How it works

1) Create a moment

Create a moment for a timeframe, occasion, or experience – e.g., “Mood in October” or “First Words (Child).”

2) Add snapshots

Add small data points: a number, a text, a feeling, an observation. You combine two categories (e.g., When + How much).

3) Complete & visualize

When the moment is finished, you close it. Visli generates a clear, beautiful chart: “That was my moment.”

Who is Visli for?

For people who want to consciously capture things – occasionally instead of constantly, aesthetically instead of spreadsheets. No account, no pressure — a quiet space for personal meaning.
