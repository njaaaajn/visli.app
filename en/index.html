Moments, not endless trackers

Visli is a place to preserve single moments of life — not for analysis or optimization, but as small, finished chapters. A number, a word, a feeling — everything can be a data point. In the end, you get a visual that says: “That was my moment.”

Yes. Export visuals as images and share if you want — no cloud required.
